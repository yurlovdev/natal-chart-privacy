# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: January 2026_

Natal Chart – Placidus respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected and used when you use the app.

## Information Collection and Use

The app does not require account registration and does not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, or contacts.

Birth data (date, time, and place) entered in the app is used **only locally** to calculate the natal chart and is not transmitted or stored on external servers.

## Analytics and Diagnostics

The app uses Firebase services provided by Google to improve stability and performance:

- **Firebase Crashlytics** — collects anonymized crash reports, stack traces, and diagnostic information such as device type, operating system version, and app state at the time of a crash.
- **Firebase Analytics** — collects anonymous usage data and interaction events to help understand how the app is used and to improve functionality.

This data does not identify you personally and is used solely for app functionality, analytics, and stability improvements.

You can learn more about Google’s data practices here:  
https://policies.google.com/privacy

## User-Submitted Information

The app provides an optional feedback and crash reporting feature that allows users to voluntarily submit information to help improve the app.

Any information submitted through this feature is sent to **Firebase Firestore** and may include user-entered text and technical details related to the app state at the time of submission.

This information is used solely for support, debugging, and improving the app. It is not used for advertising or tracking purposes.

## Data Sharing

The app does not sell, trade, or share personal data with third parties.

## Children’s Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
